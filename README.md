# marketplace_analysis
Pivoting from web development to data analysis/data-science. Here is my first project using SQL. 
This project analyses an E-Commerce dataset to identify high-value customers at risk of churn, understand where revenue is coming from and find opportunities to increase customer lifetime value(LTV).

#Key Questions Answered:
How many high-value customers are at risk of churn?
Which city has the highest average order value(AOV)?
What % of revenue comes from the top 10% Of customers?
Which platform drives the highest 6-month LTV?

#Key Insight:
19 high-value customers at churn risk. This may have an impact on revenue. 
Top 10% customers contribute 17% of revenue. 
AOV is consistent across cities. 
Platform LTv is similar. Hence, there is no dominant platform.

In conclusion, revenue is diversified and stable. However, there is an untaapped opportunity to grow high-value customers(VIP). Also, Retention should focus on high-value segments.

All SQL scripts are organized in order of analysis.
